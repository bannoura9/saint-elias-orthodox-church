Weekly Bulletin

Everything you need for the coming Lord's Day — the schedule of services, the appointed readings, the saints we commemorate, and the news of our parish family.

Home› Weekly Bulletin

Each week the parish publishes a bulletin to help you pray along and stay connected — whether you are in the pews on Sunday morning or following from home. It gathers, in one place, all that shapes the coming week in the life of Saint Elias.

What's inside each week

The weekly bulletin is a small companion to the Divine Liturgy. Whether you're new to Orthodox worship or have prayed here for decades, it helps you follow the service and know what is happening in the parish.

  • The Sunday schedule — Orthros (Matins), the Divine Liturgy, and any additional services or feast-day liturgies during the week.
  • The epistle & gospel readings — the appointed scripture readings for the day, so you can read and reflect before and after the Liturgy.
  • The saints & feast commemorated — whose memory the Church keeps that day, with a few words on their life and witness.
  • Announcements — upcoming events, ministry news, coffee-hour hosts, and opportunities to serve.
  • Prayer requests — the living and departed of our community whom we hold up in prayer, that we may pray for one another by name.
