# Saint Elias Orthodox Church — Website (Proposed Redesign)

A proposed new website for **Saint Elias Antiochian Orthodox Church**, Arvada, Colorado.

**Live preview:** see the GitHub Pages link shared with you (Settings → Pages).

## What this is
A complete, mobile-first redesign of the parish website — built around what visitors
actually need: service times and address up front, a warm welcome from Fr. George
Shawareb, a "what to expect on your first visit" guide, livestream, events/fundraisers,
and simple stewardship. Designed in an *ancient-yet-warm* Orthodox visual language
(parchment, oxblood, liturgical gold) with full light/dark support and bilingual
English/Arabic touches.

## Pages
- **Home** (`index.html`)
- **Plan Your Visit** (`visit.html`) · **Becoming Orthodox** (`new-members.html`)
- **About & Clergy** (`about.html`) · **Contact & Directions** (`contact.html`)
- **Services & Schedule** (`services.html`) — incl. bilingual Holy Week table
- **Events & Fundraisers** (`events.html`) — Golf Scramble, New Year Party, Annual Festival
- **Ministries** (`ministries.html`) · **Sunday School** (`sunday-school.html`)
- **Give** (`give.html`) · **Weekly Bulletin** (`bulletin.html`) · **Volunteer** (`volunteers.html`)
- **Watch Live & Sermons** (`watch.html`)
- **Privacy Policy** (`privacy.html`) · **Terms of Use** (`terms.html`)
