15th Annual Mediterranean Festival

A two-day celebration of Mediterranean music, food & faith — everyone is welcome, with free admission for all.

Home › Events › Festival

Each summer, Saint Elias Orthodox Church throws open its doors for a two-day, family-friendly celebration of the many cultures of the Mediterranean — a feast of music, food, and hospitality shared with all of Arvada.

Hosted at the church on 7580 Pierce St and spilling out into St. Elias Park, the festival brings together authentic Arabic music, Mediterranean cuisine, and traditions carried across generations. Come hungry, come curious, and bring the whole family — from the youngest to the eldest, there is something here for everyone. And it's all free to enter.

What to Expect

Two days of music, flavor & welcome

A taste of the Mediterranean in the heart of Arvada — here's what awaits you at the festival.

Arabic Music

Live, authentic Arabic music fills the grounds — the heartbeat of the celebration, with rhythms made for dancing.

Mediterranean Cuisine

Savory dishes and fresh-baked pastries prepared by our parish families, drawing on flavors from across the Mediterranean.

Turkish Coffee

Sip a small cup of rich, aromatic Turkish coffee — a warm tradition of Mediterranean hospitality.

Flavored Hookah

Relax with friends over flavored hookah — a leisurely Mediterranean pastime, and a fine way to linger into the evening.

Henna Art

Have delicate henna designs traced onto your hands — a beautiful, time-honored Mediterranean craft for all ages.

Fashion Show

A colorful fashion show celebrating dress and style from many cultures across the Mediterranean.

Meet Fr. George · Church Tours

Meet Fr. George Shawareb, who shares the history and traditions of the Orthodox Church and welcomes visitors on guided tours.

Family & Kids at St. Elias Park

Bring the children to St. Elias Park for activities and open space to play — a festival made for the whole family.

Plan your visit

The Mediterranean Festival is held at Saint Elias Orthodox Church, 7580 Pierce St, Arvada, CO 80003, with family activities continuing at nearby St. Elias Park. Admission is free — simply come as you are and enjoy the day.

The festival returns each summer, spanning two days of food, music, and fellowship. Exact dates change from year to year and are to be confirmed — check back closer to the season, or contact the parish to be sure of the dates before you plan your visit.

Free admission · everyone welcome

No ticket needed. Food, Turkish coffee, and treats are available for purchase on-site, and every purchase supports the parish and its ministries.

Volunteer or sponsor

A festival this size is only possible because parishioners and friends give their time each year — شكراً to everyone who pitches in. We especially need volunteers for every festival: cooking, serving, setup, and welcoming guests. Sign up to volunteer for the festival, or support the parish year-round through giving & sponsorship.

Come & See

We'll save you a seat at the table

Whether you come for the music, the food, or simply to meet your neighbors, there's a warm welcome waiting at the Mediterranean Festival. Reach out with any question, or explore our other parish gatherings.
