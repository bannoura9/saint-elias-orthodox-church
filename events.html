15th Annual Mediterranean Festival

📍 7580 Pierce St & St. Elias Park, Arvada 🎯 A two-day celebration — open to all

Our flagship event: a two-day, family-friendly celebration of Mediterranean traditions, music, and food. Enjoy authentic Arabic music, Mediterranean cuisine, Turkish coffee, flavored hookah, henna art, a fashion show, and more — with contributions from many cultures across the Mediterranean. Meet Fr. George Shawareb, who shares the history and traditions of the Orthodox Church, then head to St. Elias Park for the whole family.

Free admission

No ticket needed — everyone is welcome. Food, coffee, and treats are available for purchase on-site, all supporting the parish.

Why We Gather

More than a fundraiser

These events are woven into the life of Saint Elias. The dollars they raise are real and needed — they help fund our Sunday School and youth programs, our choir and outreach, and the everyday upkeep of the building where we pray. But they are more than a line in the budget. Around a festival table, on the golf course, and at the New Year banquet, lifelong friendships are made, newcomers become family, and the doors of the parish swing wide open to all of Arvada. When you buy a ticket or sponsor a hole, you are not just giving — you are joining us.

Sponsor or volunteer

Want to lend a hand or put your business behind an event? We would love your help. Learn how to volunteer with us, or explore sponsorship and giving to support the parish year-round.

You're Invited

Come celebrate with us
